Pizza

Description

This pizza is an amazing pizza that will make your mouth water. Only the freshest ingredients are used.

The prep time for this meal is short. It will feed multiple people.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Mozzarella Cheese

Steps

  1. Knead the dough
  2. Make a pie shape
  3. Add the sauce
  4. Add the cheese
  5. Cook in oven
